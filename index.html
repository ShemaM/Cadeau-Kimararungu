About Me

I am a multilingual data analyst and dedicated refugees’ rights advocate with over five years of experience supporting refugees and asylum-seekers.

I've collaborated with organizations such as HIAS, RCK, RefugePoint, and contributed to research funded by the University of Oxford and British Academy.

Currently, I serve as a Policy & Research Advisor at Global Rights Defenders (GRD), advancing refugee protection and migration governance.
