About Me

I am a multilingual research consultant specializing in forced migration research, policy and community engagement. My work focuses on migration governance by conducting research that reflects lived experiences and priorities of displaced communities and transforming forced migration research into policy impact. Over the years, I had a privilege of working with esteemed humanitarian organisations such as HIAS, the Refugee Consortium of Kenya (RCK), RefugePoint, HAART Kenya, and Pamoja Trust on issues ranging from refugee protection to peacebuilding and conflict resolution to community engagement with the host communities.

Biography

With over five years of dedicated research and advocacy in forced migration, I've worked at the intersection of academic research, policy development, and grassroots community engagement.

My work focuses on understanding the complex dynamics of refugee economies, protection mechanisms, and durable solutions, with particular emphasis on the Great Lakes Region of Africa.

As a multilingual researcher (English, French, Kiswahili, Kinyarwanda), I bring unique cultural and linguistic insights to my work with displaced communities.

Professional Experience

Over the years, I have collaborated with leading NGOs serving refugees in Nairobi — including HIAS, the Refugee Consortium of Kenya (RCK), RefugePoint, HAART Kenya, and Pamoja Trust — on issues ranging from community engagement and refugee protection to peacebuilding and conflict resolution.
In addition, I have contributed as a Research Assistant and collaborator on various research projects related to forced migration, funded and/or led by institutions such as the University of Oxford’s Refugee Studies Centre (RSC), the British Academy, the University of Bergen, Clark University, American University, Samuel Hall, the Refugee-Led Research Hub, and the German Fellowship. Today I continue to apply my expertise in migration governance and refugee protection at GRD (Global Rights Defenders) where I am serving as Policy & Research Advisor.
